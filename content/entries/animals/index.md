There are 5 [cohorts](cohorts "Cohorts") of the twelve animals. They are always in the same [order](order "Order"), from Rat to Pig. The order is the number inside the hexagon.

1. Rat
1. Ox
1. Tiger
1. Rabbit
1. Dragon
1. Snake
1. Horse
1. Sheep
1. Monkey
1. Rooster
1. Dog
1. Pig
